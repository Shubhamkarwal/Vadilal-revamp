Heritage

Born in Ahmedabad.
Beloved by the World.

In 1926, Ranchhodbhai Thakar began selling ice cream from a hand-cart on the streets of Gujarat. What started as a pursuit of simple joy grew into India's most trusted frozen legacy — a brand that carries the warmth of Indian flavours to 50 countries and counting.

Every product is a conversation between tradition and innovation. Between the richness of kesar-pista and the boldness of dark chocolate. Between memory and discovery.
